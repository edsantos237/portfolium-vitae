    Since I know myself that I'm passionate about technology, and since I started watching sci-fi movies, I see not only what technology is, but also what it can be.

    This mindset has driven my interest in fields that may revolutionize how people interact with technology, bending the digital world to reality, through ubiquitous computing, extended reality, sensors, networking, internet of things and machine learning.

    My professional experience bloomed through my Master's dissertation, which paved my way to the Research \& Development environment, where I've been developing extended reality experiences, mobile applications, communication features, back-office systems, 3D scene configuration tools, and backend services, usually for industrial or learning contexts.
